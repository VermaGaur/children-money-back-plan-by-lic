About LIC Children Money Back Plan

LIC Children Money Back Plan is a non-linked, participating, money-back life insurance plan designed to secure the financial future of a child. It provides financial protection against the death of the policyholder and also helps in meeting the educational and other needs of the child.

Features

  • Flexible premium payment options
  • Death benefit to secure the child's future in case of the policyholder's demise
  • Money-back payouts at regular intervals to meet financial needs at various stages of the child's life
  • Accidental death benefit rider for added protection
  • Income tax benefits under Section 80C and Section 10(10D) of the Income Tax Act, 1961

Benefits

  • Financial protection against the death of the policyholder
  • Regular money-back payouts at specific intervals during the policy term to meet financial needs
  • Maturity benefit on survival of the policy term
  • Optional accidental death benefit rider for added protection
  • Tax benefits under Section 80C and Section 10(10D) of the Income Tax Act, 1961

FAQ

What is the minimum age to buy LIC Children Money Back Plan?

The minimum age to buy LIC Children Money Back Plan is 18 years.

What is the maximum age to buy LIC Children Money Back Plan?

The maximum age to buy LIC Children Money Back Plan is 55 years.

What is the premium payment term for LIC Children Money Back Plan?

The premium payment term for LIC Children Money Back Plan is 10, 15, or 20 years.

What is the policy term for LIC Children Money Back Plan?

The policy term for LIC Children Money Back Plan is 25 years minus the age of the child at entry.
